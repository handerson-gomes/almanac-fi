# 054 — Major purchase scenario feature

## Story

As a user, I can compare a major purchase or home scenario against the rest of my plan before committing funds.

## Requirements

Create scenario-only feature calculations for purchase cost, financing/debt payments, recurring obligations, cash-flow impact, and allocation trade-offs; add workflow/dashboard/capability/evaluations.

## Acceptance criteria

It never modifies active plan without proposal approval; comparison identifies displaced lower-priority goals; financing assumptions are explicit and not financial advice.
